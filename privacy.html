Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 21, 2026

Techzx AI Hub ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

1. Information We Collect

  • Contact form data: name, email, and message when you contact us.
  • Newsletter data: email address when you subscribe.
  • Local preferences: theme choice, favorites, bookmarks, and liked items, stored only in your browser's local storage — not sent to any server.
  • Usage data: basic, non-identifying analytics (pages visited, device type) if analytics tools are enabled.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your messages and inquiries.
  • To send newsletter updates, only if you subscribed.
  • To improve our website's content and user experience.

3. Data Storage

Favorites, bookmarks, likes, and theme preference are stored locally on your device using browser local storage. We do not have access to this data unless a backend is added in the future.

4. Third-Party Links

Our AI Tools Directory links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these external sites.

5. Cookies

We may use minimal cookies or local storage to remember your preferences. We do not sell your data to third parties.

6. Children's Privacy

Our website is intended for general and student audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please reach out via our Contact page.
